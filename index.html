How to edit
Click any text to edit it and get a toolbar with font, size, color, bold, italic, underline, align, link, and paint format.
Hover any section for background + hide buttons.
Hover between sections for the + Add block button.
Undo / Redo: Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Shift+Z
Paint format: In the text toolbar, click the brush icon to copy styles, then click another element to paste.
